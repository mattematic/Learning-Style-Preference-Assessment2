Learning Style Preference Assessment

This assessment will help you identify your preferred learning style(s) and provide personalized recommendations to enhance your learning experience.

Instructions

Rate each statement on a scale of 1-5 based on how well it describes you:

  • 1 = Strongly Disagree
  • 2 = Disagree
  • 3 = Neutral
  • 4 = Agree
  • 5 = Strongly Agree

The assessment consists of 30 questions covering different learning preferences:

  • Visual
  • Auditory
  • Reading/Writing
  • Kinesthetic
  • Deep Learning
  • Strategic Learning
